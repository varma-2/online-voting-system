Available Elections

  • Lok Sabha Elections - 2024
    Date: April 10, 2024
    Description: The Lok Sabha elections will be held to elect members to the lower house of the Parliament of India. This election is crucial for the formation of the next government.
  • Rajya Sabha Elections - 2024
    Date: May 15, 2024
    Description: The Rajya Sabha elections will be held to elect members to the upper house of the Parliament of India. This election involves indirect voting by the elected members of the Legislative Assembly.
  • State Election - 2024
    Date: June 5, 2024
    Description: The state elections will be conducted to elect members of the state legislative assembly. This election is vital for the state's governance and representation.
